Digital Wellbeing in a Management Information System (MIS) Project

Scenario

You work as a trainee Digital Well-being Specialist for a leading business organization. As a part of your role, you have to carry out a small-scale research in order to recognize a range of digital products and features which ensure a great end user experience for all at the organization. Whilst technology and the internet can simplify and enhance our lives they can also be distracting, be a cause of anxiety, and make us feel upset. Being in control of technology enables us to use its full potential and gain all the benefits of it.

igital Wellbeing is about fashioning and sustaining a healthy relationship with technology. As technology plays a big part in human lives at workplace so people find themselves spending an increasing amount of time online and on their devices. Human wellbeing is dependent upon mental and physical health and thereby digital wellbeing is influenced by online interactions and the amount of time spend on digital devices accessing information through management information system to perform business operations. Nowadays there is an increasing demand from users for healthier and calmer experiences when engaging with digital content. For example Google mail services allow users to snooze emails for later viewing.

You are expected to explore the research area

Making digital technologies accessible: how can digital products and features ensure a great end user experience for all?

from the standpoint of a prospective computing professional.

This assignment focuses to provide the opportunity for you to investigate digital wellbeing within computing systems and explore the responsibilities and solutions to the problems presented.
