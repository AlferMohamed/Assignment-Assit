Refund Policy

Effective Date: 01-01-2025

At AssignmentsAssist.com, we are committed to providing high-quality services and ensuring customer satisfaction.

However, due to the nature of our services, refunds are governed by the following terms and conditions:

Eligibility for Refund

Service Not Delivered: If we fail to deliver the requested service or product within the agreed timeframe.

Duplicate Payment: If you are charged twice for the same service, the duplicate payment will be refunded promptly.

Mis-selection of Product: Refunds are only applicable if the selected assignment has not been provisioned to the user.

Non-Refundable Cases

Customer Change of Mind: If the customer decides not to use the service after the order has been initiated or completed.

Partial Usage: If you have used or downloaded the delivered materials, a refund will not be granted.

Plagiarism Claims Without Proof: Any claims of plagiarism must be substantiated with reliable proof (e.g., a plagiarism report from a trusted tool).

Requesting a Refund

Contact our Customer Support Team via support@assignmentsassist.com

Provide your order details, a clear explanation of the issue, and supporting evidence (if applicable).

Refund requests must be submitted within 3 days from the date of service delivery.

Refund Processing

If your refund request is approved, we will process the refund within 10 working days.

Refunds will be credited to the original payment method used during the transaction.

Any delays caused by third-party payment processors are beyond our control.

Refund Policy Decision

The decision to approve or deny a refund request is solely at the discretion of the organization.

All refund requests will be carefully evaluated based on the outlined refund policy terms, and the organization's decision will be considered final and binding.

Changes to Refund Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on this page.
